Paralaxia

A short poem with no actual meanng.

Left to dwell
on morrow's yield,
Held adrift
through sifting fields.

Nigh we pondered,
mongst the naves
long we wandered,
till rainfall came

Long we drank,
the bounteous drops
Long we thanked.
lest we meet
the wanderer's grave.
